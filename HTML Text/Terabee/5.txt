REAL-TIME PEOPLE COUNTING AND EXTRACTABLE METRICS
Install Terabee’s 3D Time-of-Flight people counting systems for accurate insights as people enter or leave a building, room or space. With over 95% accuracy and embedded counting algorithms, our people counting systems give real-time and accumulative occupancy level data. We enable informed data-based, decision-making in Smart Buildings, offices, shops, higher education and hospitality facilities.

Terabee people counting solutions come ready for discrete installation above conventional to large doors or passageways. They are optimized for easy software and hardware integration with an optional analytics portal.
SEE OUR PEOPLE COUNTING DEVICE
People Counting L-XL - Real-time, privacy-protected data for wide entrances and passageways
Using advanced Time-of-Flight technology, Terabee People Counting L-XL is capable of counting multiple people passing in both directions below the device, as they enter or leave a space. It is GDPR compliant, protects anonymity and maintains 98% people counting accuracy.
DISCOVER PEOPLE COUNTING L-XL
People Counting M - Wireless real-time people counter for single doorways and narrow corridors
Detect and count people over conventional swing doors and passageways. Terabee People Counting M is discrete, compact and can be flexibly deployed via PoE, Ethernet or WiFi connectivity. GDPR-compliant, the device ensures user anonymity with 95% counting accuracy.
SEE PEOPLE COUNTING M
Key features of our Time-of-Flight people counter systems
Personal privacy protection (GDPR-compliant) as standard
Uses 3D depth sensing (no camera used)
Easy-to-install and configure via a local web interface
Reliable data with 95% or better people counting accuracy
Effective in low light environments
Direct data integration to Cloud and servers via HTTP or MQTT
Real-time and on-board computed data
Remote device management and firmware updates via Device Management Services
Power supply over Ethernet (PoE 802.3af interface), Ethernet and WiFi data transmission (for People Counting M)

WHY USE PEOPLE COUNTING SYSTEMS?
Develop Smart Building solutions via accurate people counting
Never has there been a greater need or more demand for people counting solutions in Smart Buildings. The ability to count, monitor and track people as they enter and exit buildings, rooms and spaces is now an imperative to maximize facilities’ efficiency, reduce costs and make working environments more comfortable and productive in the post-Covid era.
