Compact, rugged Time-of-Flight distance sensor
The Terabee IND-TOF-1 smart distance sensor, with six operating modes and industrial communication protocol, provides enormous versatility in a compact, cost-effective and easy-to-install package. Suitable for object presence detection and counting, object sorting for size, material level monitoring and distance measurement applications, this sensor is an ideal choice for your industrial automation tasks.
SEE PRODUCT
Lightweight, compact industrial-grade multi-pixel thermal camera
Terabee IND-Thermal-90 is one of the smallest industrial-grade multi-pixel thermal cameras on the market. With its small footprint, affordability and large (90°) Field of View, Terabee IND-Thermal-90 is an ideal component in Industry 4.0 and Industrial IoT set-ups, especially for applications where previously thermal measurement monitoring was too expensive. Applications such as machine and equipment monitoring, production process and area monitoring, and preventative explosion or fire detection are ideally suited for Terabee IND-Thermal-90.
SEE PRODUCT
Industrial heat monitoring made simple
The Terabee IND-Thermal-90 offers cost-effective continuous monitoring to eliminate the need for manual spot checks. This sensor embeds onboard intelligence, provides EDGE data treatment and local feedback loops to minimize the need for external computation.
