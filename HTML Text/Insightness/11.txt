Insightness Sensor in the News
Posted on July 23, 2019
The Swiss National Television (SRF) recently visited the befriended research group of Prof. Davide Scaramuzza, the “Robotics and Perception Group” at the University of Zurich where they featured a drone with two of our Silicon Eye sensors.
The full article (German) and a video can be found here:

https://www.srf.ch/news/panorama/forschung-an-drohnen-drohne-weicht-fussbaellen-aus-dank-zwei-kamera-augen
