CVPR Talk Online
Posted on July 23, 2019
Stefan Isler, our Head of Software recently gave an exciting talk at the Computer Vision and Patter Recognition (CVPR) Conference; one of the top conferences in the field.
The talk announced out latest vision sensor, the Silicon Eye 2 which we will release later this year.
The full talk and the slides which were part of a workshop on event-based vision organized by the Robotics and Perception Group (RPG) of the University of Zurich, can be found on the organizer’s homepage:
Talk:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9IJwF9xYEoU&feature=youtu.be
Slides:
http://rpg.ifi.uzh.ch/docs/CVPR19workshop/CVPRW19_Insightness.pdf
Further Workshop Material:
http://rpg.ifi.uzh.ch/CVPR19_event_vision_workshop.html
