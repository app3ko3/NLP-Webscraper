List
A list of companies operating in the fields of autonomous robotics, drones and precise indoor positioning and navigation with the focus on the industrial applications.
AUTONOMOUS ROBOTS
Industrial delivery
https://6river.com/ – robot “Chuck” – boxes delivery in warehouses
https://clearpathrobotics.com/ – different robots, including, autonomous delivery. Probably, they deliver other people’s robots (Jun.2021)
https://locusrobotics.com/ – autonomous mobile robot (AMR) for warehouses (Jun.2021)
https://twinny.ai/home – autonomous delivery robots for logistics/manufacturing. S.Korea (Jun. 2021)
https://www.vecnarobotics.com – tugs. Not much known (Jun. 2021)
https://www.forwardx.com/ – very well funded Chinese autonomous delivery company (Dec.2021)
  Outdoor delivery
https://eliport.com/ – lesser-known 4-wheeled small grocery delivery robot
https://www.nuro.ai/ – prominent autonomous 4-wheeled mini-cars for grocery delivery. Uses a LIDAR. (Jun. 2021)
https://robby.io/ – another small 6-wheeled delivery robot for grocery. We know little about it (Jun.2021)
https://www.starship.xyz/ – notable player with a small 6-wheeled grocery delivery robots. Estonia/USA (Jun. 2021)
Research and university
https://www.leorover.tech/ – a research robot-rover. Also have built-in integration and support of Marvelmind Indoor “GPS” (May. 2022)
https://www.agilex.ai – different types of autonomous robots and parts (chassis, etc.). China (Jun. 2021)
https://www.donkeycar.com/ – an opensource DIY self driving platform for small scale cars
https://www.leorover.tech/ – different kind of research and special (Fukusima) robots. Poland (Jun. 2021)
https://robotnik.eu/ – several types of robots. But looks like suitable mostly for research
  Service robotics (cafe, hospitals, advertising and similar)
http://www.aethon.com – a more traditional and established player with autonomous delivery robots focused on hospitals (Jun. 2021)
https://promo-bot.ai/ – advertising robot-guide. Russia (Jun.2021)
https://pudurobotics.uk/ – robots for cafes and restaurants. Uses Intel RealSense. Est. 2016 in Shenzhen (Nov. 2020)
https://www.savioke.com/ – old friends with hotel delivery robots (Jun. 2021)
https://www.segwayrobotics.com  – several types of robots from personal mobility to industrial delivery
  COMPONENTS AND OTHER PARTS

Arms
https://www.universal-robots.com – well-established robot arms vendor (Jun.2021)
INS
https://www.kvh.com – precise inertial solutions for marine, industrial, robotics (Jun.2021)
Other parts
https://www.taoglas.com – An interesting provider of antennas for UWB, connectors and other IoT solutions in general (Nov.2020)
INDOOR POSITIONING
There are several technologies relevant for precise indoor positioning and autonomous robots, drones for industrial applications. Our highest focus is on the most applicable ones – UWB. Much less – on BLE or WiFi or Lora or ZigBee. We may list some players in those fields as well despite, but mostly because they stand out from the crowd by something.
BLE, WiFi and other
https://www.esri.com – large maping player that is buying other IPS companies (Jun.2021)
Optical
https://antilatency.com/ – inside-out tracking for VR/AR, filming (Jun.2021)
https://www.optitrack.com/ – well-known optical tracking (outside-in?) for VR/AR, filming, drones indoor (Jun.2021)
Other types and special
– http://magnettrack.ru/en – reels-based positioning for industrial applications (Jun.2021)
Ultra Wideband (UWB)
https://www.airfinder.com – not entirely clear what their solution actually is (Jun.2021)
https://www.decawave.com/ – de facto, the main UWB chip supplier for the majority of the UWB players. Now part of qorvo (Nov.2020)
https://eliko.ee/ – UWB solutions moving more to general IoT (Jun.2021)
https://estimote.com/ – A former BLE solution vendor turned to UWB solution vendor as well (Jun. 2021)
https://www.indoorway.com/ – UWB solutions… Not clear which hardware (Jun.2021)
https://www.infsoft.com/ – active player in Indoor Navigation and Positioning – UWB and several other technologies (Jun. 2021)
https://www.inpixon.com/ – mostly BLE and WiFi, but listing UWB as solutions as well (Jun.2021)
https://kinexon.com – UWB for industry and sport. Not so clear (Jun. 2021)
https://www.nxp.com/ – Potentially, a very big UWB chip player. But their business model in regards to UWB is not clear (Nov.2020)
https://pozyx.io/ – popular small UWB player. Arduino-friendly
https://real-trac.com/en/ –
https://www.redpointpositioning.com/ – UWB solutions. HW+SW. USA/China/Ukraine (Jun.2021)
https://rtloc.com/ – A seemingly small UWB player (Jun. 2021)
https://www.ubitrack.com – UWB solutions with reasonable product prices (Jun. 2021)
